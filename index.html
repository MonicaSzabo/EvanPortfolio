About Me

I love creating environments for video games because I get to build new and interesting worlds for people to explore, worlds born in the imagination where anything can happen.

I find a lot of inspiration from films like Tron, and Pacific Rim, games like Half-Life 2, and Bioshock, animators like Hayao Miyazaki, and places like Disneyland. I am inspired by the richness and the beauty of the worlds they create.

As a kid I never expected my video game modding hobby to ever become a career, but when I discovered that you could get a degree in video games I knew that's what I wanted to do, and I never looked back.

"I have had the pleasure of working with Evan for almost 6 years. He is honestly one of the hardest workers I have ever come across during my many years in the industry. Evan is pretty much everything you want in an environment artist; he is fast, imaginative, and focused on quality. He is considerate of others and makes work with that in mind so anyone can pick up his work if needs be and know exactly what he was thinking. I would gladly work with Evan again anytime."

-Karl Holbert, Art Lead at KingsIsle Entertainment
